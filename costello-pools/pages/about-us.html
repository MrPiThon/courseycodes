Welcome to Costello Pools

At Costello Pools, we believe that a backyard pool is more than just a place to swim—it's a place where memories are made. As a family-owned and operated business, we've been serving our community with pride and passion since the early 1990s.

Our Story

Costello Pools was founded by Jane and Michael Costello, a husband-and-wife team with a dream of bringing top-quality pools and exceptional customer service to our neighbors. What started as a small venture from our garage has grown into a beloved local business, known for our personal touch and dedication to excellence.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing Costello Pools means choosing a company that stands by its work. Our satisfied customers are a testament to our dedication and integrity. We are not just building pools; we are building lasting relationships.

Thank you for considering Costello Pools. We look forward to making your backyard dreams come true!
